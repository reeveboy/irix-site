Get ready for a thrilling experience as the Department of Computer Science presents the highly anticipated Inter Collegiate Tech Event, back after a 2-year hiatus due to the pandemic. Join us for two days filled with excitement, laughter and thought-provoking activities. Unleash your tech skills and show off your knowledge as you compete with students from other colleges. With plenty of fun and learning opportunities, this event promises to be a memorable one. Mark your calendars and gear up for an unforgettable experience!

General Rules

  • The event is open to all enrolled college students.

  • Teams should consist of a maximum of 15 number of members. (excluding futsal team & gaming)

  • All participants must adhere to the principles of fair play and sportsmanship. Any form of cheating or disruptive behavior will not be tolerated.

  • Participants must follow all safety guidelines and instructions provided by the event organizers.

  • Teams must report at the designated time and location specified in the event schedule.

  • Teams that arrive late may be penalized or disqualified, at the discretion of the event organizers.

  • Teams must pay a registration fee of ₹2,250 for a team of 15 students. i.e. ₹150 per student. For futsal a registration fee of ₹500 per team;

  • By participating in the event, participants agree to hold harmless the event organizers and their affiliates from any and all claims, losses, damages, or injuries.

  • All teams should have a Bonafied letter from the Principal

  • All participants should compulsory carry ID cards
