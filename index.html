IRIX 2023

Get ready for a thrilling experience as the Department of Computer Science presents the highly anticipated Inter Collegiate Tech Event, back after a 2-year hiatus due to the pandemic. Join us for a day filled with excitement, laughter and thought-provoking activities. Unleash your tech skills and show off your knowledge as you compete with students from other colleges. With plenty of fun and learning opportunities, this event promises to be a memorable one. Mark your calendars and gear up for an unforgettable experience!
