Futsal

Description

Get ready for a knockout competition where only the strongest teams will survive! The first 16 teams to register will be in the running for the top prize.

The competition will follow a thrilling knockout system, with each match lasting 10 minutes per half and a 3-minute break in between.

The draw will be revealed on the day of the event, adding to the excitement. Teams must report at 9:30 a.m. sharp, or risk being disqualified.

Only one team per college is allowed to participate, and the competition is open to all students, including those who are not studying IT.

Unfortunately, re-entries will not be allowed.

Teams must consist of 8 players, with 5 players on the field and 3 substitutes.

The registration fee is Rs.500 per team.

To ensure a fair competition, players must not be participating in any other events.

Get ready to show off your skills and compete against the best!
